Teaching Philosophy

I am committed to delivering rigorous and engaging education in Economics and Data Science. My teaching approach emphasizes the practical application of theoretical concepts through concrete examples and active learning methods (flipped classroom, project-based evaluation). I strive to make abstract notions accessible, particularly in technical fields like econometrics and programming (R/Python).
